Our Facilities

Class Rooms

We have 28 spacious classrooms for students of all branches with good ventilation and incoming natural light. Each classroom accommodates 60 students comfortably.

Computer Lab

Our computer lab is well configured with 60 computers with internet connection to enable students explore the subject more deeply. Our computer lab is first of its kind in the town of Kavali with 60 computers and always active internet connection.

Seminar Hall

Our seminar hall is capacious enough to accommodate 150 students at a time. It is equipped with audio and projector for presentations tarmacking a professional path for student’s careers.

Library

We have a huge library of 5000 volumes lodging huge knowledgebase for students to gain exposure to the worldly knowledge at once place. Out students are allowed to use the library for unlimited hours without any restrictions.

Physics Lab

Our physics lab is equipped with all the apparatus needed to do experiments and nurture practical education. At the laboratory, students will conduct experiments to cross check the theoretical knowledge with practical outcomes
